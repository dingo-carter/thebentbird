Distillery & Speakeasy

San Francisco, CA

The Bent Bird is the only high-end speakeasy bar in San Francisco, bringing the vintage charm to the energetic Castro District.

During the day as a distillery we hold tasting events, distillery tours, and afternoon lounge. At night, we bring the party with three different dance floors each with different music genres, private parties, and VIP bar and club access.
